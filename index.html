A Legacy of Authentic Chinese Cuisine

Authentic Heritage

Mr Zo Chinese Restaurant brings the authentic flavors of traditional Chinese cuisine to Wilton. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, ensuring every dish maintains its cultural integrity and exceptional taste.

Family Tradition

Founded with a passion for sharing the rich culinary traditions of China, we pride ourselves on creating a warm, welcoming atmosphere where families and friends can gather to enjoy exceptional food and create lasting memories.
